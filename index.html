Hi! Considering you're reading this, you've found my website! My name is Nnenna and I'm currently a Sophomore computer engineering student at Texas A&M University.

From Houston, Texas, I'm passionate about the semiconductor industry and chip design. I'm focused on creating accessible, high-performance technology solutions that put cutting-edge computing in everyone's hands. Through my involvement in research labs, engineering competitions, and leadership roles in professional organizations, I'm gaining practical experience in semiconductor design, AI applications, and hardware systems.

Beyond academics, I love exploring new music—whether it's catching Tate McRae or Billie Eilish in concert—spending quality time with my siblings, and discovering new eateries. These moments of fun and connection fuel my creativity and drive in engineering!

Feel free to explore my journey throughout university and reach out if you'd like to connect ♥
